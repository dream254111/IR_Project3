compare rect angular coordinates of the same stars meas ured on two different overlapping photo graphs without computing right ascensions and declinations For instance Donner used this method for strengthening his determina tion of plateconstants in his reduction of the astrophotographic catalogue plates Sur le Rattachement des cliches astrophotograph iques Acta Soc Sci Fenn Tom XXI No 8
